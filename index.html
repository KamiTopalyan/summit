8th Hisar Virtual Coding Summit

Hisar Coding Summit, which was created to develop the potential of our students within the scope of digital transformation, will be held for the 8th time this year online on 1-2-3 April 2022. Hisar Coding Summit will be in both English and Turkish making it an international event with 16 workshops, 9 speakers, and a project fair.
